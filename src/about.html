About Us

Learn more about the Veterinary Teaching Hospital at Usmanu Danfodiyo University, Sokoto

Our History

The Veterinary Teaching Hospital at Usmanu Danfodiyo University, Sokoto was established in [YEAR] to provide clinical training for veterinary students and quality veterinary services to the community.

Over the years, we have grown to become a leading veterinary facility in Nigeria, equipped with modern diagnostic and treatment equipment to handle various animal health conditions.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional veterinary education, research, and clinical services to advance animal health and welfare in Nigeria and beyond.

We are committed to training competent veterinary professionals, conducting innovative research, and delivering high-quality clinical services to the community.

Our Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all aspects of our work, from education to clinical services.

Compassion

We treat all animals with compassion and respect, ensuring their comfort and well-being.

Innovation

We embrace innovation and continuous learning to improve our services and education.
